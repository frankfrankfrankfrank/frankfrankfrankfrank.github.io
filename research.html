Data is the oil of the 21st Century

UX research helps discover what users find valuable, as well as what frustrates them. Data and insights are valuable at every phase of the product cycle, but have the most impact when research is applied at the start.

Only user testing right before or after deployment can generate a large number of findings that would be difficult for any development team address. When research is part of the discovery process, the bad news doesn't feel as insurmountable and the good news inspires. This has a compounding effect on the project as the team sheds ideas causing friction and repeats successful ones.
